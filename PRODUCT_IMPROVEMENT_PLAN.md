# Product Improvement Plan

Last updated: 2026-07-01

## Customer-language correction

Do not position this product as answer generation. Customer language points to
realistic practice, role-specific follow-up pressure, pacing, confidence, and
using prepared evidence without looking like someone is reading canned AI text.

Use language such as:

- practice under realistic pressure;
- use your own stories;
- interviewer-style follow-up questions;
- prep mode, not cheating mode;
- evidence-grounded reminders;
- pacing and clarity review.

## Product gap

The current app already has a strong real-time overlay and Story Log concept.
The product gap is packaging and proof: the public repo needs a clearer roadmap,
examples, and a capability story that separates ethical preparation from live
answer generation.

## Capability backlog

1. Add public examples of interview packet structure, Story Log output, and
   session notes with synthetic data.
2. Add a practice mode spec for role/company-format simulation and follow-up
   question pressure.
3. Add a STAR/story-bank improvement path tied to resume evidence.
4. Add scorecard examples for clarity, evidence strength, role alignment,
   structure, pacing, and follow-up quality.
5. Clarify "prep, not cheating" in README and release notes.

## Acceptance criteria

- Public materials show the app using prepared evidence, not inventing answers.
- Examples avoid real interview, employer, or personal data.
- Roadmap distinguishes live overlay support from mock-practice features.
- Ethical and legal consent boundaries remain visible.
